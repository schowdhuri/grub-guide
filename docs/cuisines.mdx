# Browse by Cuisine 🍽️

Explore the food collection organized by culinary traditions. Each cuisine showcases its unique flavors, cooking techniques, and cultural significance.

## Featured Cuisines

### 🇹🇭 Thai Cuisine
**The Art of Balance** - Thai cuisine is renowned for its perfect harmony of sweet, sour, salty, spicy, and bitter flavors. From aromatic curries to refreshing salads, each dish represents centuries of culinary refinement.

**Explore**: Curries • Noodles • Salads • Soups • Street Food • Desserts

---

### 🇵🇭 Filipino Cuisine
**Comfort and Heritage** - Filipino food reflects the country's rich history through bold flavors, hearty stews, and unique preparations. Each dish tells a story of family, tradition, and resilience.

**Explore**: National Dishes • Soups • Regional Specialties • Street Food • Desserts

---

### 🇻🇳 Vietnamese Cuisine
**Fresh and Fragrant** - Vietnamese cuisine emphasizes fresh herbs, balanced flavors, and delicate textures. From aromatic phở to crispy bánh mì, each dish celebrates freshness and simplicity.

**Explore**: Soups & Broths • Rice Dishes • Spring Rolls • Street Food
