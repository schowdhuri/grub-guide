**Negros Grilling Tradition**: Inasal evolved from **traditional Visayan grilling methods** that emphasized marination, charcoal cooking, and basting techniques that create complex flavors through patient preparation.

**Bacolod Innovation**: The city's version gained fame through **specific marinade combinations** and annatto oil basting that created distinctive flavors setting it apart from other regional grilled chicken preparations.
