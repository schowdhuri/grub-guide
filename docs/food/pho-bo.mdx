## Regional Variations

### Northern Style (Hanoi)
- **Clear, subtle broth** with restrained seasoning
- **Wider noodles** and minimal garnishes
- **No bean sprouts** or extensive herb plates
- **Purist approach** focusing on broth quality

### Southern Style (Saigon)
- **Sweeter broth** with added rock sugar
- **Abundant fresh herbs** and garnishes
- **Smaller noodles** with more toppings
- **Hoisin and sriracha** commonly provided
