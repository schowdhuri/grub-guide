## The Art of Tiny Perfection

Unlike large manti, chuchvara are **tiny dumplings**‚Äîno bigger than a thumbnail when sealed. Making them requires skill and patience, as each miniature parcel must be properly sealed with minimal filling.

**Two Ways to Enjoy**: Chuchvara can be served in **light clear broth** (chuchvara shorva) or **boiled and served with vinegar** and sour cream for dipping.
