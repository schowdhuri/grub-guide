## The Architecture of Perfection

### The Foundation: Vietnamese Baguette

**Crispy Outside, Airy Inside**: Vietnamese baguettes are **lighter and airier** than French originals, with a thinner, crispier crust and more open crumb structure.

**Size Matters**: Vietnamese baguettes are typically **shorter and wider** than French baguettes, creating the perfect sandwich-to-filling ratio.
