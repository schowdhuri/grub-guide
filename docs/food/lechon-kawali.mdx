## Cooking Technique & Science

**Two-Stage Cooking**: Success requires **boiling then frying**‚Äîthe boiling tenderizes meat and firms skin, while deep-frying creates the signature crispy exterior through rapid moisture removal.

**Skin Preparation**: Achieving perfect crispy skin demands **thorough drying** after boiling, often requiring hours of air-drying to remove surface moisture that prevents proper crisping.

**Oil Temperature Mastery**: The deep-frying stage requires **precise heat control**‚Äîhot enough to crisp skin quickly but controlled enough to prevent burning while ensuring meat heats through.

## The Crispy Skin Science

**Moisture Elimination**: Perfect crispy skin requires **complete surface moisture removal**‚Äîachieved through boiling (which tightens skin) followed by thorough drying before the final frying stage.

**Temperature Precision**: Success depends on **oil temperature management**‚Äîtoo low results in greasy skin, too high burns the exterior before proper crisping occurs throughout.
