## The Art of Assembly

### Wrapping Technique

**Professional Method**:

1. **Rice paper preparation** - Brief softening, not soaking
2. **Filling placement** - Strategic positioning for even cooking
3. **Tight rolling** - Firm enough to prevent bursting
4. **Edge sealing** - Egg white or water for adhesion
5. **Resting period** - Allows wrapper to firm up

## Technical Excellence

### Professional Tips

**Wrapping Secrets**:

- **Moisture control** - Rice paper should be pliable, not wet
- **Filling ratio** - Proper proportion prevents bursting
- **Edge overlap** - Sufficient wrapper overlap for sealing
- **Uniform size** - Consistent cooking requires consistent sizing

**Frying Perfection**:

- **Oil quality** - Fresh oil for clean flavor
- **Temperature consistency** - Maintains proper crisping
- **Timing precision** - Prevents overcooking

### Common Mistakes

**Avoiding Pitfalls**:

- **Overfilling** - Causes wrapper rupture
- **Underwrapping** - Results in loose, messy rolls
- **Wrong oil temperature** - Too hot burns, too cool makes greasy
- **Overcrowding pan** - Reduces oil temperature
