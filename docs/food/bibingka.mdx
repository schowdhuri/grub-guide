## Essential Components & Traditional Construction

### Rice Cake Foundation
- **Rice flour (galapong)** - Traditional ground rice base
- **Coconut milk** - Rich liquid creating tender crumb
- **Fresh milk** - Additional richness and moisture
- **Eggs** - Structure and golden color development
- **Sugar** - Balanced sweetness complementing savory toppings

### Signature Toppings
- **White cheese** - Salty contrast to sweet base
- **Salted duck egg (itlog na maalat)** - Traditional protein topping
- **Grated coconut** - Fresh texture and natural sweetness
- **Margarine or butter** - Richness and golden color

### Traditional Preparation
- **Banana leaves** - Natural cooking vessel and flavor infusion
- **Clay pots (palayok)** - Traditional cooking method
- **Charcoal heating** - Authentic heat source for proper texture
- **Top and bottom heat** - Achieving golden crust with tender interior

### Modern Adaptations

- **Metal pans** - Modern vessel alternatives
